Notes

In this slide, we learned information about Einsteinium. Einsteinium is a synthetic radioactive metallic element with atomic number 99 and atomic mass 252. It has 99 protons, 99 electrons, and 153 neutrons. Einsteinium belongs to the Actinide series and is located in period 7, group 3 of the periodic table. Understanding the properties and characteristics of elements such as Einsteinium is crucial in various fields such as chemistry, materials science, and nuclear engineering.
