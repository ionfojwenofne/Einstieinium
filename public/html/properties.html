Einsteinium Properties

Einsteinium has no known color in its elemental form.

Einsteinium has no known odor in its elemental form.

Texture

Einsteinium has no known texture in its elemental form.

State of matter at room temperature

Einsteinium is a solid at room temperature.

Melting point

The melting point of einsteinium is 1133 °C (2071 °F).

Boiling point

The boiling point of einsteinium is 1269 °C (2316 °F).

Flammability

Einsteinium is not flammable.

Reactivity

Einsteinium is a highly reactive element and must be handled with great care.

Notes

In this slide, we learned information about Einsteinium. Einsteinium is a synthetic radioactive metallic element with atomic number 99 and atomic mass 252. It has 99 protons, 99 electrons, and 153 neutrons. Einsteinium belongs to the Actinide series and is located in period 7, group 3 of the periodic table. Understanding the properties and characteristics of elements such as Einsteinium is crucial in various fields such as chemistry, materials science, and nuclear engineering.
